Our Story



At Everglow & Co., our story is rooted in the passion and vision of our founder, Erika Thomas. With a deep love for creating moments of joy and serenity, Erika embarked on a journey to bring warmth and light into the lives of others. Inspired by the beauty of nature and the power of scent, she dedicated herself to crafting candles that not only illuminate spaces but also evoke emotions and memories. Each candle, meticulously hand-poured with the finest ingredients, reflects Erika's commitment to quality and her unwavering belief in the transformative power of fragrance. Join us on this fragrant adventure and discover the essence of Everglow & Co..
